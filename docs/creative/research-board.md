## Awwwards Research Board

| Reference | URL | Category | Distinctive idea | Pattern to borrow | Details to avoid |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Forms and Semantic Forms | https://www.awwwards.com/awwwards/collections/forms-and-conversational-interfaces/ | Forms | Minimal interfaces that simplify user input. | Reduce the intro to fast, scannable steps. | Decorative contact-page styling that hides the task. |
| Interactive Multistep Form | https://www.awwwards.com/inspiration/interactive-multistep-form | Multistep form | One focused decision at a time. | Treat each assessment question as a clear step with progress context. | Slider gimmicks or ambiguous controls. |
| IKEA Family natural-language form | https://www.awwwards.com/inspiration/ikea-family-natural-language-form | Natural-language form | Human, conversational framing. | Use plain, direct copy for level selection and answer prompts. | Brand-specific illustration and parallax language. |
| Form hover animation | https://www.awwwards.com/inspiration/form-hover-animation-denys-verholomchuk | Form microinteraction | Sticky form structure with field feedback. | Add subtle option hover/selected states and sticky actions. | Heavy hover-only behavior on touch devices. |
| Engaged | https://www.awwwards.com/sites/engaged | Agency site/forms | Crisp transitions and two-color accent palette. | Use restrained violet/cyan accents and decisive panel transitions. | Vortex/page-transition spectacle in a work form. |
| Mobile excellence checklist | https://www.awwwards.com/mobile-excellence-guidelines.pdf | Mobile usability | Forms should fit the viewport, use legible type, and keep CTAs visible. | Tighten spacing, improve tap targets, keep core action reachable. | Any motion or layout that slows completion. |
