# Assessment Form Motion Blueprint

- Signature principle: calibration, not spectacle.
- Intro interaction: panel and chips appear with a short upward reveal.
- Level selection: selected card tightens border, background, and indicator immediately.
- Progress behavior: meter width animates as the learner advances.
- Question transition: question panel fades and rises slightly on step change.
- Hover behavior: answer card border and background respond subtly on pointer devices.
- Mobile alternative: keep all information visible; sticky action replaces any animated affordance.
- Keyboard behavior: focus rings are visible on level cards, answers, and CTA buttons.
- Reduced motion: no entrance/question tweens; selected and progress states update instantly.
- Performance fallback: all effects use transform, opacity, border, and background; no layout-heavy animation.
